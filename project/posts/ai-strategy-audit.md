## What the audit is for

An AI strategy audit is useful when a team already feels the pressure to adopt AI but does not know where it will actually create leverage.

The deliverable is not a trend report. It is a ranked build list.

## What I inspect

- Existing workflows and repeated manual decisions.
- Internal tools, data access, and privacy constraints.
- Places where a model can assist without becoming a hidden source of truth.
- The cost of maintaining the workflow after the first demo.

## The output

At the end, the team gets a short map: what to build first, what to avoid, what can be tested in a week, and what needs real engineering ownership.
