## The shape

Embedded builder is a one-day-a-week format. I join the team long enough to understand the actual workflow, then ship small internal tools with the people who will maintain them.

## Why it works

Most AI consulting fails when advice and implementation separate too early. The build changes the advice. The advice changes the build.

## Good fit

- Teams with clear internal bottlenecks.
- Founders who want a working prototype before a larger investment.
- Engineering teams that need a practical AI pair, not a vendor presentation.

## Bad fit

This is not the right format for vague transformation work. It works best when there is a real workflow, a real user, and a clear owner.
