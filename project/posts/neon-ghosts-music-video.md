## The brief

NEON GHOSTS started as a short test: one track, one color system, one character silhouette, and no more than a weekend of production.

The goal was not to let the model make every decision. The goal was to build a repeatable pipeline for AI music-video ideas.

## The pipeline

1. Generate the track direction and lyrics.
2. Lock the visual vocabulary before generating clips.
3. Produce short shots, not full scenes.
4. Edit like normal footage.
5. Add analog noise only after the rhythm works.

## The lesson

AI video becomes more useful when you stop asking for the whole film. Ask for the pieces you can direct, reject, and assemble.

Short clips gave me control. Long clips gave me surprises I could not edit around.
